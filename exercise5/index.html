Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g parmesan
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta, add pancetta and egg mix.
  5. Stir quickly and serve hot!
